# Synthesize Findings

## When to use it

Use this skill when raw material is messy and the goal is a quick research handoff.

## Workflow

1. Extract the strongest evidence from each source.
2. Group overlapping ideas so the summary does not repeat itself.
3. End with unresolved questions, risks, or missing references.

## Output expectations

- Lead with findings, not process.
- Separate observed facts from inferred conclusions.
- Keep the handoff short enough for a teammate to scan in one pass.
