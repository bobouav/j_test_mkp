# Shape Campaign Brief

## When to use it

Use this skill when a content idea exists but the framing, hook, or structure still feels loose.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the audience, promise, and main action the content should drive.
2. Produce a few distinct angles instead of minor variations on one idea.
3. Refine the strongest option into a concise draft direction.

## Output expectations

- Prioritize clarity and differentiation.
- Avoid filler language and generic marketing claims.
- Keep the final direction specific enough to draft from immediately.
