# Triage Incident

## When to use it

Use this skill when the team needs a clear incident summary more than a deep root-cause analysis.

## Workflow

1. Capture the triggering event, impacted surface, and known severity.
2. Separate confirmed facts from assumptions or early hypotheses.
3. End with the next operator actions and any missing information.

## Output expectations

- Lead with impact and scope.
- Keep the incident handoff short enough for an on-call teammate to scan quickly.
- Call out blockers that prevent confident escalation.
